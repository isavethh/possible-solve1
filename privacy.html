How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for public health purposes:

  • Outbreak Detection: Aggregated sales patterns help identify potential outbreaks 2-3 weeks early
  • Risk Mapping: Location data enables geographic risk visualization for health authorities
  • Trend Analysis: Historical data improves our AI prediction models
  • Alerts: To send you relevant zone-specific health alerts
  • Research: Anonymized, aggregated data may be used for epidemiological research
Who We Share Data With

We share data only with:

  • Health Authorities (SEDES): Aggregated, zone-level reports (not individual pharmacy data)
  • Research Partners: Anonymized data for peer-reviewed epidemiological studies
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS) with strict data protection agreements

ðŸš« We NEVER share: Your pharmacy's individual sales data, personal contact information, or any data that could identify specific pharmacies in public reports.

How We Protect Your Data
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using AES-256 in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access limits who can view data
  • Anonymization: Data is anonymized before aggregation and analysis
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are audited quarterly
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what's necessary
  • Local Compliance: Data stored in compliance with Bolivian data protection laws
Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access Request a copy of your data
Correction Update inaccurate information
Deletion Request account and data deletion
Portability Export your data in standard format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pulsedengue.org

Data Retention
  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • After Deletion: Personal data deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized Data: May be retained indefinitely for research
  • Backups: Removed from backups within 90 days of deletion
Children's Privacy

PULSE DENGUE is intended for use by licensed pharmacies and is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
