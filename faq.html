How do I register my pharmacy?
To register your pharmacy:

1. Download the PULSE DENGUE app
2. Tap "Register New Pharmacy"
3. Fill in your pharmacy details (name, address, zone)
4. Upload your SEDES license number
5. Accept the terms and submit

Your registration will be reviewed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email once approved.

What if I don't have internet?
No worries! PULSE DENGUE works offline:

1. Fill out your report as usual
2. The app saves it locally
3. When you connect to internet, it syncs automatically

You'll see a "Pending Sync" badge until the report uploads. Reports can be stored offline for up to 7 days.

What time should I submit reports?
We recommend submitting between 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM based on yesterday's sales. This gives you time to review your inventory and ensures data reaches health authorities early.

You can set a daily reminder in Settings to help you remember.
